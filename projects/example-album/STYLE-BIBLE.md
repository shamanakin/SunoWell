# Style Bible - Signal Repair (example)

## Palette words (8 to 12)

- crisp
- warm
- airy
- tight drums
- pulsing sub
- subtle glitch
- melodic motif
- modern clean mix
- intimate vocal pocket

---

## Instrumentation

Must-have:
- tight drums
- warm bass or sub
- one repeating synth motif

Allowed:
- electric guitar accents (light)
- piano motifs (sparse)
- light vocal chops (rare)

Banned:
- trap hats as the main texture
- orchestral brass
- acoustic guitar as the main bed

---

## Mix and space

- Target mix: modern
- Vocal pocket: intimate but forward
- Texture: mostly clean, light grit allowed on 2 tracks max

---

## Approved variation knobs

- arrangement density (sparse vs full)
- rhythmic complexity (simple vs syncopated)
- vocal intensity (intimate vs confident)
- texture (clean vs light grit)
- tempo within band
