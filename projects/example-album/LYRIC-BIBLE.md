# Lyric Bible - Signal Repair (example)

## POV and voice

- POV: first person
- Voice: direct, grounded, specific

---

## Themes

Primary:
- rebuilding focus without moralizing
- refusing to outsource identity

Secondary:
- attention as a home you return to
- learning to sit with discomfort

---

## Motifs (rotate)

- signal vs noise
- repair vs replace
- window of tolerance
- hands on the wheel

---

## Language constraints

Avoid:
- generic motivational slogans
- vague abstractions without images
- "we will rise" style filler

Prefer:
- concrete nouns (hands, screen, streetlight, breath, keyboard)
- specific actions (mute, delete, pause, return, rebuild)
- short chorus lines with strong vowel sounds

---

## Chorus rules

- Chorus line count: 4
- Hook rule: chorus contains a short repeatable anchor phrase
- Repeat rule: first chorus line repeats in each chorus

---

## Rhyme and density

- Rhyme density: low to medium
- Allowed rhyme style: loose
