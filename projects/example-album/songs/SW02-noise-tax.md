## Intent

Name the cost of distraction as a real, daily tax, with a punchy chorus that invites people to recognize their own pattern.

---

## Style constraints

- Palette words: tight drums, pulsing sub, hypnotic synth riff, modern clean mix
- Tempo target: 114–118 BPM
- Must-have elements: punchy drums, hypnotic riff, confident vocal pocket
- Ban list: no trap hats as main texture, no acoustic guitar bed

---

## Lyric constraints

- POV: first person
- Theme: distraction as a hidden cost you pay with your life
- Tone: sharp, clear, controlled
- Motifs allowed: noise tax, hands on the wheel
- Clichés banned: vague hustle slogans
- Structure: Verse (8), Chorus (4), Verse (8), Chorus (4), Bridge (4), Chorus (4)
- Hook rule: the first chorus line is the hook and repeats
